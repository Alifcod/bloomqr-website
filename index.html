Frequently asked questions
Got questions? We have answers.
Do customers need to download an app? ▾
No. Customers just scan the QR code with their phone camera and the ordering page opens instantly in their browser. No app download needed.
What devices do I need? ▾
Any phone or tablet with a browser works — iPhone, Android, anything. For the kitchen screen, any phone or cheap Android tablet will do. You don't need a computer.
Can I change my menu anytime? ▾
Yes, instantly. Go to Owner → Menu, add or remove items, and changes appear on the customer ordering page within seconds.
Does it work without internet? ▾
BloomQR requires an internet connection to sync orders between customer phones and the kitchen screen. A basic mobile data plan is sufficient — it uses very little data.
How do I get my QR code? ▾
After setting up your stall, go to Owner → Settings → scroll down to "Your QR code". You can download it as an image or print a ready-made poster directly.
Is it only for burger stalls? ▾
Not at all. BloomQR works for any food business — restaurants, cafes, food trucks, night market stalls, canteens, and more. You define your own menu and categories.
